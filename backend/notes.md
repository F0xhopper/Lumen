Requirements

- User will be able ask broad and specific questions about thomism from theology to metaphysics to ethics and more.
- Users will be able to click on the sources sited in the answers to go straight to the source material.
- Users will be able to ask follow up questions about the answers they receive.
- The system will be trained on:
  - Primary sources of thomism (e.g. Summa Theologica, Summa Contra Gentiles, etc.)
  - Secondary sources of thomism (e.g. de veritate, de ente et essentia, commentary on De Anima etc.)
- The system will be able to provide citations for the sources it uses in its answers.
- The system will first and foremost use St Thomas Aquinas' own words and ideas to answer the questions.
- The system will give significatly better answers to questions about thomism than a general purpose language model like GPT-4, due to its specialized training on thomistic sources.
